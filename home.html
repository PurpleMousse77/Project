Maintenance

Periodic maintenance also falls under the general class of home repairs.

  • Check HVAC system filters.
  • Look for leaks around toilets and sinks.
  • Inspect grout and caulking.
  • Check kitchen vent hood filter.
  • Test smoke and carbon-monoxide detectors.
  • Stroll around outside.
  • Watch for ice dams.
  • Test your sump pump.
  • Close foundation vents.
  • Cover outdoor air-conditioning units.

Cleaning

  • Ceiling fans and light fixtures (dusted)
  • Blinds and window sills (dusted)
  • Baseboards (dusted)
  • Outside of appliances cleaned /inside microwave
  • Stove-top and backsplashes (washed)
  • Mirrors (Windexed)
  • Tub/Shower and tiles cleaned
  • Wastebaskets emptied
  • Beds made (includes master and 1 additional bed)
  • Stairs (vacuumed, wood dusted)
  • Floors (vacuumed and /or mopped)
